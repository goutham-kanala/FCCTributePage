Padmashri Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao(NTR)

(Man of masses)

Time Line of NTR's Life

  • 1923 - Born in Gudivada,AndhraPradesh
  • 1940 - Completed metriculation
  • 1947 - Left Job as Sub-registrar
  • 1949 - Started his film career with a walk-on role as a policeman in Mana Desam
  • 1968 - Received Padma Shri award from Government of India
  • 1982 - Found Telugu Desam Party(TDP)
  • 1983 - NTR sworn in as the 10th and the first non-Congress Chief Minister of the state AP on 9 January
  • 1985 - Dissovled Assembly and re-elected as CM of AP state for second time
  • 1989 - He was voted out of power due to a wave of anti-incumbency sweeping the state as a result of which the Congress returned to power
  • 1994 - NTR returned to power for a third and final time in the December
  • 1996 - NTR died of a heart attack
